How to Play

Introduction

Welcome to Bot Battle Arena! In this game, you will program your bot to compete in a battle arena. The goal is to write Python scripts to control your bot's behavior. Use your programming skills to outsmart other bots and win the game!

Writing Python Scripts

Each bot follows a script written in Python. You can define how your bot moves, turns, scans for enemies, and attacks. Here's a basic example:

def on_tick(bot): if bot.scan(): bot.fire() else: bot.move_forward() bot.turn(10)

In the example above:

  • bot.scan(): Checks for nearby enemies.
  • bot.fire(): Fires a projectile.
  • bot.move_forward(): Moves your bot forward.
  • bot.turn(10): Turns your bot 10 degrees clockwise.

Bot API

Here are the key functions you can use in your script:

  • move_forward(): Move your bot forward.
  • turn(degrees): Rotate your bot by the given degrees.
  • scan(): Check for enemies in front of your bot.
  • fire(): Fire at an enemy in range.
